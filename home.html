Dr. Abhijit Raghuwanshi has done M.D. (Dermatology, Venereology & Leprology) from Smt. N.H.L, Medical College, Ahmedabad. After finishing his degree, he has worked in prestigious institutions like Lokmanya Tilak Medical College, Sion, Mumbai & Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, Nerul (As Lecturer, Skin Department, Rajawadi Hospital).


He is practising as Consultant Dermatologist, Cosmetologist & Trichologist in Thane since many years. He is also attached as consultant dermatologist at Indian Red-cross Society Hospital, Thane. His areas of specialization are clinical dermatology & cosmetology including chemical peels & lasers.
