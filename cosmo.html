COSMETOLOGY

Cosmetology (derived from Greek “kosmetikos “means "beautifying") is the art or practice of the beautification of the body (including skin, hair, or nails). It deals with the use of cosmetics/other products & procedures to enhance beauty of a person.

In todays highly competitive world, external appearance of a person makes a huge difference. With the help of different cosmetological procedures, we can see apprciable improvement in skin blemishes.
